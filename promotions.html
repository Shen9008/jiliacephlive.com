6 promo red flags to watch for

  1. Vague game eligibility

    If excluded games aren’t listed, ask support before you wager big on a single title.

  2. Extreme playthrough

    Very high multiples can make a large headline bonus impractical for casual play.

  3. Short expiry

    Weekend-only windows pressure rushed decisions—prefer offers that match your real schedule.

  4. Payment method exclusions

    Some e-wallets or vouchers are excluded from welcome deals—verify in the cashier FAQ.

  5. Caps on winnings

    Free spin wins sometimes cap cashout—know the ceiling before you celebrate.

  6. Stacking rules

    Combining promos can be blocked; clearing one before starting another avoids forfeits.
