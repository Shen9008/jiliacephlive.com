Warning signs

Consider stepping back if you chase losses, hide play from people you trust, borrow to bet, or feel anxious when you’re not gambling. These patterns are common—and help is available.

Practical tools

Many licensed operators offer deposit limits, loss limits, session timers, and cool-off periods. Use them before you need them. Official JiliAce cashier pages typically list what’s available in your region.

Self-exclusion

Longer breaks through self-exclusion can be the right move. Ask the operator’s support team how local rules apply and what reinstatement looks like.
